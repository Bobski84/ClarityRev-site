Common Questions

How are you different from a lead gen agency?

Agencies usually sell you a list of leads or book meetings for you using their black-box methods. We are GTM Engineers. We build the system inside your organization (or on your behalf) so you own the data, the infrastructure, and the IP.

Do you work with our existing SDRs?

Yes. We often act as the "Technical Lead" for SDR teams. We provide them with better data and automated signals so they can focus on selling rather than prospecting.

What tools do you use?

We are tool-agnostic but have deep expertise in the modern GTM stack: Clay, Apollo, Instantly, Smartlead, HubSpot, Salesforce, Make.com, and Zapier.

How long is an engagement?

Our typical GTM Engineering builds take 3 to 6 months. We design, build, stabilize, and then either transition to a maintenance retainer or hand off to your internal team.
